About Me

My name is Courtney, a professional graphic designer with over seven years of experience in print design. I have recently developed an interest in front-end web development and aspire to explore the world of coding to discover my calling in this field. My enthusiasm for computers and technology has been a driving force behind my passion for this industry. I am eager to leverage my existing design skills and knowledge to create innovative and dynamic web designs. I am excited to embark on this new journey and to see where it takes me as I continue to integrate my design expertise with my evolving technical abilities.


When I am not working, my interests include video games, film, and cosplay. I hope to get into streaming games on twitch soon. I have always had a love of gaming and horror!
